Refund & Cancellation Policy

How registration fees are handled in the event of cancellation.

Template — last reviewed June 2026. Please confirm the policy with the organizing committee before launch.

Non-refundable fees

Registration fees for ICCCMLA 2026 are non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances, except where the conference is cancelled in its entirety by the organizers (see below). Fees are exclusive of taxes and actual bank / payment-gateway charges.

Cancellation by the organizers

If the conference is cancelled in full by the organizers, paid registration fees will be refunded. Bank, tax, and payment-gateway charges already incurred are non-recoverable and may be deducted.

Change to online-only mode

The conference is hybrid. If circumstances require a change to online-only mode, the event will still take place and fees remain non-refundable; no kits or meals are provided for online participation.

Duplicate or erroneous payments

For duplicate or clearly erroneous payments, contact us within 7 days at convenericccmla@gmail.com with proof of payment; verified cases will be refunded net of transaction charges.

Contact

For any refund query, email convenericccmla@gmail.com.
