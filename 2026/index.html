01About the conference

Where intelligent data is the backbone of the next engineering transformation.

ICCCMLA is a collaboration and networking platform for researchers and scientists working in cybernetics, cognition, intelligent computing, and machine learning. Alongside paper presentations, it runs Innovation and Startup tracks plus industry panels.
