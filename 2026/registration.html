Registration & Proceedings

Fee categories and terms for ICCCMLA 2026, plus the published proceedings of past editions.

01Please note

Before you register.

  • Registration fees do not include accommodation — please make a separate accommodation request.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present for the paper to be included in the proceedings.
  • Virtual presentation is allowed; no kits or lunch are provided for virtual presentation attendees.
  • Limited student travel grants are available for in-person attendees.
  • Accompanying persons should register under the Listeners / Attendees category separately.
