About the conference

The 2026 IEEE 8th International Conference on Cybernetics, Cognition and Machine Learning Applications — ICCCMLA.

01Overview

Where intelligent data is the backbone of the next engineering transformation.

In this era of smart devices and smart machines, ICCCMLA is a collaboration and networking platform for researchers and scientists working in cybernetics, cognition, intelligent computing, and machine learning. Alongside regular paper presentations, the conference runs Innovation and Startup tracks.

Industry professionals join focused panels on the commercial use of these technologies across banking, healthcare, insurance, information management, and defense. Special tracks for Young Professionals and Women in Engineering are also part of ICCCMLA 2026.
